Frequently Asked Questions

What is Griffins ?

Collaborating with Reena
The Reena Developers site offers APIs to external companies and developers, facilitating implementation using a variety of technologies developed by Reena.

How Griffins Works

Griffin operates by leveraging large language models like the Reena AI Model to respond to user prompts with information it has learned. However, it's crucial to remember that Griffin isn't human; it lacks independent thought or emotions and can't replace human relationships or professionals. While it can provide assistance, it's not a substitute for human interaction or personal responsibility.

Why can Griffin get things wrong ?

Generative AI and all of its possibilities are exciting, but it’s still new. Griffin will make mistakes. Even though it’s getting better every day, Griffin can provide inaccurate information, or it can even make offensive statements.

Be mindful of how you use Griffins

You have an opportunity to shape Griffin for others. Griffin will use the conversations you have to improve future responses for everyone so don’t enter anything you wouldn’t want a reviewer to see or Google to use.

Can Griffin help with coding ?

Nope, Griffins cannot help with coding or topics about coding. You are responsible for finding coding assistance elsewhere and should exercise caution and careful review of all code for errors, bugs, and vulnerabilities before relying on it. Code may also be subject to an open-source license, and Griffin provides related information.
